Welcome

If you are viewing this on a VR-compatible device you will see a VR headset icon in the lower right. Click it to enter VR!

Controls

Rotate: To rotate your view in VR, briefly tap either thumbstick to the left of right. (Or just turn your physical body!)

Teleport: To teleport to a different location in the space, push either thumbstick forward and hold. With the thumbstick pushed forward, point your controller to any part of the floor. Release the thumbstick to teleport to that location.
